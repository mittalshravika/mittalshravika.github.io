About Me

I am a Software Development Engineer at Adobe, India, as part of the Adobe Illustrator Team. The areas that interest me the most revolve around Social Network Analysis, Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning.

I completed my undergraduate studies from IIIT-Delhi majoring in Computer Science and Engineering (with honors). I was awarded the Chancellor’s Gold Medal for the best academic performance. During my B.Tech. I have worked with Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty as part of the Lab for Computational Social Systems on various projects around Network Science, Graph Mining, and Social Network Analysis. I have also worked with Dr. Pushpendra Singh as part of the Mélange Lab where I was involved in developing human computer interaction frameworks for different use cases. In addition to this, I have some industrial research experience through internships at IBM Research, India and Adobe, India.
